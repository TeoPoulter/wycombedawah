About Wycombe Da'wah

This work is simple in its aim: to call people to Allah, answer questions, remove misconceptions about Islam, and support sincere people in finding the truth.

Donate Now Any questions?

What we do

Every week, our team of trained brothers from Wycombe go out and engage non-Muslims in conversation, answer their questions, and clarify the message of Islam. By the permission of Allah, people accept Islam through these efforts, and the work continues to grow.

Why it matters

Many brothers now active in da'wah once simply stopped at a table as non-Muslims, had a conversation, and were guided by Allah to the truth.

How long this has been going

The da'wah team has been serving the community for nearly 20 years, and with the current wider team of around 10 brothers, the output is only increasing.

Real da'wah work

Your support goes towards ongoing, local grassroots da'wah work, not just ideas or intentions.

Growing effort

The work has long been supported through small ad hoc donations and by brothers from the team, but the need is growing.
